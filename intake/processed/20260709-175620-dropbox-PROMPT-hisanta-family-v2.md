# Hisanta — mastery engine, grandma rail, family gifting

Target repo: hisanta.
Source specs: REVIEW v1 §6 (H1–H4), agreed unchanged in v2/v3. Depends on queued F1–F3; F1 constitution verdicts (deny charge_child / open_ended_child_chat, escalate loot/gift/AI-message) are locked constraints.

## Objectives

1. **H1 Mastery engine** — spaced-repetition learning quests (reading, math, kindness/social) with adaptive_difficulty (F3) tuning challenge; rewards on earned/effort-contingent schedules (weekly quest completion → advent door); efficacy metrics surfaced to parents. Constraint: no variable-ratio reward schedules coupled to purchases or gift triggers (F1-consistent; constitution test). Proof: scheduler tests; constitution test.
2. **H2 Grandma rail** — elder-relative surface: daily 2-min recorded story slot, guardian-approved milestone reaction feed (no child PII, F2 opaque-edge pattern), one-tap gift funding into parent-controlled queues. Proof: PII-free serialization test; approval-gate test.
3. **H3 Kindness quests, parent-verified** — real-world acts verified by parent as oracle mint reward coins; no AI judgment of child behavior. Proof: mint requires parent-verification receipt (fail-closed).
4. **H4 Family gifting protocol** — ad-hoc + advent + earned-reward lanes unified under parent approval (F1 escalation); relative-funded match jars (grandma matches earned coins). All purchase authority adult-side. Proof: authority tests fail-closed.
5. **N5 School mode** — teacher-facing classroom deployment of H1: class-level quests, family-visible progress, teacher dashboard; cohort onboarding flow. COPPA/consent flows for school context [MATERIAL]. Proof: classroom fixture end-to-end test.
