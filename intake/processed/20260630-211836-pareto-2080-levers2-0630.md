- id: irs-float-optimizer
  title: Stop the interest-free loan to the IRS — right-size withholding / estimated tax
  material: no
  model: sonnet
  depends: []
  proof: `node --test tests/irsFloatOptimizer.test.js` exits 0
  prompt: |
    Build `server/utils/irsFloatOptimizer.js` (pure logic) that computes how much a user is
    over-withholding / over-paying estimated taxes and the opportunity cost of that float sitting
    with the IRS interest-free until refund. Inputs: ytd withholding, projected liability (reuse
    taxOptimizer/taxAdvantaged where useful), safe-harbor thresholds (110%/100% of prior year,
    90% of current), filing status, remaining pay periods, baseline yield. Outputs: target
    per-period withholding to land just above safe harbor, projected refund eliminated, dollars
    freed, annualized opportunity-cost recovered (freed × yield × avg months held), and a
    makeExplanation rationale. Guard against UNDER-withholding (never recommend below safe harbor;
    flag penalty risk). TEST tests/irsFloatOptimizer.test.js: an over-withholder gets a lower
    target + positive recovered $; a user already at safe harbor gets "no change"; never recommends
    a target that drops below the safe-harbor floor.
