PROJECT: pareto-2080

# W19 — The internal economy + global optimization + deep-tech bets. This tier makes the platform an
# ECONOMY: everything users could share, pool, trade, or co-own — material goods, access/memberships,
# negotiated deals, activities/goals, knowledge, liquidity, risk — clears on ONE internal market
# priced in a single unit of account (RETIREMENT-DAYS, the cash<->time exchange rate that already
# exists in server/utils/valueLedger.js + retirementMonteCarlo.js). Goal: make every user
# structurally ~1000x better off than acting alone, at all times, by capturing utilization pooling,
# fixed-cost amortization, volume, orthogonal-preference matching, knowledge reuse, risk pooling, and
# liquidity netting — then splitting the surplus fairly. Builds on ALL prior engines (W16/W17/W18);
# import them, do NOT rewrite; compose W17/W18 engines "when present" with graceful fallback.
# Pure-logic ESM, no external deps, seedable RNG (mulberry32). node:test proofs. Conventions:
# parseInt+isNaN before Prisma; requireAuth + requireUserIdOwnership in handlers; anonymize at WRITE
# time; HMAC signing reuses agentLedger key; money in USD, TIME in retirement-days. Test gate:
# `npm test` must not regress beyond the 2 known pre-existing failures (agentLedger, bookingSaga).
